RGB, Glowing Edges

This is no ordinary fish in the glowing card sea — it’s the rare kind that doesn’t just float… it glows with style. 🐠✨

Hooked on my linkedin catch, I decided to cast my net wider and reel in a card with shimmering edges and a subtle color fin... I mean border.

I’m using a mesh gradient background (think rainbow trout but more CSS), wrapped in radial gradients to give it that elegant edge-glow. Then it’s masked again with a conic-gradient that swims after your pointer — like a curious goldfish that thinks your cursor might be food.

This technique feels much smoother than the usual splash of radial-glow, because this glow clings to the edge like a clingy clownfish — naturally and with a touch of drama.
